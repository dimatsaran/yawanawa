Rates

The costs for each customized retreat vary based on the length of time, number of ceremonies, number of participants, and location. As an example: the cost for a private Soma Immersion retreat with 10-15 people in Mexico will be $ 2,200 / person. This includes accommodation, food, the ceremonies, yoga and meditation classes, sharing and integration circles, sound healing, energy healing, and much more.

Contact us at mail@sacredjourney.earth for more information.

SacredJourney.Earth keeps your customized retreat completely confidential.

Reservation and Cancellation:

We hold customized retreats for a minimum of 10 and a maximum of 15 participants. Smaller groups are possible for the price of a retreat of 10 people.

Don’t have 10 people? Then integrate your smaller group with the participants of our scheduled retreats. See our Retreat Calendar to learn more about our dates.

To reserve a private retreat, we ask you to make a deposit of 50% of the full retreat price at least 10 weeks in advance. The other 50% is to be paid 2 weeks before the first day of the private retreat.

If you cancel or change any part of your reservation at least 60 days before the first day of your retreats, a $ 75,—per-person processing fee will be charged and the remaining balance will be refunded.

There are no refunds for cancellations made within 60 days before the first day of a retreat.

SacredJourney.Earth reserves the right to cancel any retreat at any time. If your retreat is canceled, we will notify you and offer you the following options:

• You may receive a full refund

• Subject to availability, you may transfer your private retreat to another period
