Mission. Values and Practices

Mission

A New Earth is Rising

Our Mission is to make a global impact on the way we live and heal ourselves. Together we can build one global conscious community, built on love and compassion.

SacredJourney.Earth is founded on the belief that transformation of consciousness is the basis for transformation on this Earth; individually, collectively and as a society. We believe that by uplifting consciousness we nurture the seeds for a new Earth to blossom; a place where we can all live in community and harmony with each other and nature.

We seek to be a platform and a catalyst for your transformation, through providing education, a daily spiritual practice, natural medicine, transformational retreats along with emotional and moral support in your journey towards health, happiness and unlimited potential.

Values

Transformation of consciousness is the basis for transformation of the world; individually, collectively and as a society.

A daily spiritual practice is a cornerstone for personal growth and transformation.

Human possibilities are unlimited.

Realization of the human potential transcends political, religious and scientific dogma.

All share the potential to love, learn, feel, and create.

Strong natural tools of transformation can be used in a space that is held with love, protection, wisdom and compassion. The intention of using those tools are healing, growth and the uplifting of consciousness.

Personal attention is a catalyst for growth, hence our choice to work in small groups.

Practices

Gratitude. The attitude is gratitude. We recognise and honour the gifts of every single moment.

Service. We offer our service by offering our experience and support to the growth of others.

Growth. We provide learning opportunities and support personal, collective and social transformation. We take risks to discover and reveal ourselves. We share the right to uplift our consciousness and to realize our full potential to love, to feel, to manifest and to learn, living fully in this moment, here and now.

Responsibility. We take responsibility for who we are; our choices, our words, attitudes and actions. We recognise that our actions affect others and acknowledge our part in each situation.

Integrity. We strive for harmony among our words, actions, values and beliefs and to foster conscious communication and self-expression.

Beauty. We offer beauty in our work as much as we can. Beauty allows for trust to come in, trust allows for safety to come in. To feel safe allows the growth and healing to happen.

Space. It is our intent to hold a safe space, where people feel guarded and protected to go deep inside of themselves with the purpose of growth and healing.

Confidentiality. We are grateful for all that is shared with us and honour and respect that in full confidentiality.

Sexual Safety. Your sexual safety is guaranteed at all times in all our works. So you can open and blossom towards your full potential, in complete trust and surrender.
