Integration and Embodiment after Sacred Ceremony

I have spend almost 2 decades and over 10.000 hours of teaching, research and personal experience on the path of healing and raising consciousness.

With all my experience and I designed this course for you if you are looking for ways to integrate and embody your ceremonial experiences into the biggest ceremony of all: Daily Life. So you can experience the fruits of them in a life where you feel happy, radiant and connected to your dreams and visions..

Integration is as important — if not more important — than the healing that happens in a Sacred Ceremony.

Integration and embodiment is what happens after your Sacred Ceremony; taking those teachings, healing and insights into the biggest ceremony of all; Daily life.

A one of a kind course for integration and embodiment after Sacred Ceremony on the market. In these 5 modules discover how to:

– Prepare your physical body to for work with the Spiritual energies in Sacred Ceremony

– How to build the power to manifest your visions and dreams

– Heal from past trauma

– Keep up the radiant energy experienced after Sacred Ceremony

On top of that you will receive a bonus module where I tell you about the encounter I had with a big Snake in the Amazon of Brazil during one of my long dieta’s with the Yawanawá, and share the teachings that I received from her on how to plant a seed of something you would like to see in our life, and how to nurture it until it has come into this reality; how to beam the future.

Also you will receive information on post-ceremony diet, and general practices & guidelines that will help you with the integration and embodiment of your ceremonial experiences into daily life.
