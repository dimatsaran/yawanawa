WEEKEND RETREAT

a Journey in Ojai, California

We are honored to invite you to our home in Ojai, California for a special mini-retreat complete with 3 sacred circles (2 night, 1 day), lodging, food, sharing, integration, meditation and much more.

The Ojai Valley is a special place 30 minutes inland from the coast of Santa Barbara and Ventura nestled in between the mountains. The energy is very peaceful, nourishing and uplifting.

During your stay, you will be nourished with delicious vegetarian food, good company, fresh air, and open space.

We are deeply dedicated to our practices, academic studies and teachings. Using our experience, we will provide support and guidance throughout your Journey towards health and full potential. The focus of this retreat will be on self-empowerment, healing, and personal transformation. Join us for this unique experience!

Ojai Weekend Details

Length: Friday – Sunday
Maximum Capacity: 8 – 10 people

Day 1: A Gentle Introduction A Warm Welcome! We will come together in our home between 3PM and 5PM and begin to get to know each other. Each person will speak with Dennis before we come together in a circle to begin our first evening together.

Day 2: A Day of Nourishment and a Night of Deep Healing We will begin the day with Kundalini Yoga and then spend the rest of the day enjoying and relaxing. In the evening we will come together for a round of sharing and integration, followed by our 2nd ceremony – a night of deep healing.

Day 3: Celebration of Life For this last day we will celebrate the light and life with a daytime ceremony. Movement, expression and exploration make for a beautiful and inspiring day and a smooth transition into your daily life.

3 days of deep healing and awakening on all levels
