6 – DAY LUXURY RETREAT

We are honored to offer you a Sacred Journey in the ancient home of Quetzalcoatl – The Feathered Serpent God. The ancient and Sacred Valley of Tepoztlan, where the spirit blossoms in the mountains. Tepoztlan is a particularly fertile mystical space which will allow to soften the senses and relax you in a way that empowers the potential of the Grandmother.

You will be nourished with a delicious array of vegetarian and vegan meals, as well as Yoga and Meditation classes in beautiful yoga studio. Beautiful double suits are surrounded by an Ashram, a Stupa, several baths and a Pyramid Built with the same scale and orientation as the Pyramid of Cheops, in Egypt, located on an area which carries the highest vibration found so far in Mexico, measured by Indian scientists and NASA.

Soma Immersion Details

Length: 6 days
Maximum: 15 people

Day 1: A warm welcome! In the evening we start with a sharing/introduction, followed by the first ceremony: The Sound Journey.

Day 2: Kundalini Yoga & Meditation, a round of Sharing & Integration & the second ceremony: A Night of Deep Healing.

Day 3: Kundalini Yoga & Meditation, Sharing & Integration, Rest. Night of Music and Meditation.

Day 4: We will start the day with a integrative Kundalini Yoga and Meditation class. A round of sharing and integration followed by the third Ceremony: connecting with nature.

Day 5: Sharing & Integration. Daytime Closing Ceremony, to bring our energies back together, fully grounded in the body. We start the ceremony in a deep way, and will come out of it in celebration of life, in enjoyment of our time and each other.

Day 6: Sharing & Integration, Closing lunch together.

6 days of deep healing and awakening on all levels
