Feeling into joining one of our retreats, but have some questions?

Craving for a listening ear and some inspirational words?

Private teachings, that sounds like what I want!

How do I stay committed to a daily practice?

If you feel any of these things, then you have come to the right place. I am here to help you in any way I can during our personal consultation sessions.

The sessions will happen online by audio or video conference using ZOOM, a very easy to use system. You will receive a link, and by clicking that link on basically any device, you will enter our meeting without having to install a program. You can either book 1 session or you can purchase a package of several sessions. A package will give us the opportunity to work together over an extended period of time, allowing us to get to know each other better, and to deepen our work step by step.
