Dennis’ Story

In November 2014 Dennis, the founder of Sananda-Wasi, went to visit his spiritual family, the Yawanawa Indians who live deep in the Amazon Rainforest surrounded by untouched land. During that time, he learned about the traditional medicines of the Amazon. For one of the first times in their history they opened up their knowledge about their Medicinal Plants to a Western Person. Read below more about the Journey of Dennis.
