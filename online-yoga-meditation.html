Online Yoga & Meditation

A Personal Daily Spiritual Practice is the cornerstone of your Sacred Journey. There is almost no bigger gift that you can give to yourself then a daily spiritual practice.

There are 2 main keys to a Daily Spiritual Practice:

- To connect regularly with it: do it on a daily basis, and develop a regular sequence.

- Find a Practice that resonates with you.

SacredJourney.Earth offers you a simple and easy way to build your Daily Spiritual Practice anywhere, anytime and on any of your devices. Just sign up, choose one of the videos and start!

A Daily Spiritual Practice cultivates concentration and awareness, builds and strengthens your physical body, calms the mind and opens your heart. A Daily Spiritual Practice will also strengthen you nervous system. Your nervous system is like an antenna that receives spiritual energies. When that antenna is strong it will be much more easy to receive those energies, when that antenna is weak, it can be difficult for the body to receive those energies. A Daily Spiritual Practice will lead you onwards on your Journey towards health, happiness and unlimited potential.

We hold private ceremonies with a minimum of 2 consecutive ceremonies.

Going into Sacred Ceremony can be a very beautiful and deep process, allowing you to connect deeper within yourself, your heart, your soul and all that is. In my experience in the last years, seeing many people go through the process of Sacred Ceremony, I learned that it is not always supportive to give somebody a deep experience in 1 night and then send them home.

If you come from your daily life, with a busy mind, the stress, the travel, the organization, and dive right into a deep ceremony, that can be very disorientating experience. I decided to stop offering only 1 Sacred Ceremony to people. It is just not supportive in my view and experience in working with Sacred Ceremony. We hold private ceremonies with a minimum of 2 consecutive ceremonies.

We will start with a first Ceremony that is on the gentle side. Will you feel the power of Sacred Ceremony during that night? Yes you will. It will be gentle, allowing you and your body to relax, your mind to calm down from daily chatter, and your heart to open for time to come. After that first night there will be much more trust, on levels conscious and unconscious to you.

That will be an excellent preparation for the second Sacred Ceremony the following day. It will allow for a night that can be more profound. What does going deeper in Sacred Ceremony mean? To go deeper means that you can visit deeper layers of self and consciousness. There are several layers of consciousness that can be accessible in Sacred Ceremony. To reach those layers takes time, trust and the right guidance.

There is the possibility to add a 3rd ceremony as well (optional). Having gone deeper during the second night, the 3rd ceremony, which can be done during the day depending on setting and location, will allow us to start deep, and then to come out in celebration. In that ceremony we might move a bit, and use our bodies to do some yoga or dance. Moving your body really helps your to come back into your body, to come back into this reality, preparing you to go back into the biggest ceremony of all: daily life.

In these times, in the Western world, we are used to have everything quick, fast, strong, now, deep. Spiritual work doesn’t happen that way; it takes time for your system to slow down and to relax. When the body is more relaxed it can receive in ways that might not have seen possible before.

We will be there for you in any way we can during your private ceremonies.
